About Us

Our Approach

At Event, we empower people to connect, explore and create meaningful experiences through events. Whether attending or organising, our platform is designed to support continuous discovery, collaborationg and celebration across all event type - from professional conferences to community fundraisers and private parties. By combining creativity, usability and efficiency, we enhance the experience for every partcipant and seamlessly connect them with a vibrant ecosystem of opportunity.

Our Vision

We aim to simplify how people gather, engage and organise events, enabling seamless connection between attendees and hosts. With a holistic view of technology, we build tools that are both powerful and intuitive - desinged to support users at every step. Our commitment to practical innovation and personal support fuels the long-term success of our community and partners.
